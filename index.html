Welcome to LMSC

LMSC is one of the world's leading independent marine surveying companies, providing a wide portfolio of services in cargo, nautical and technical matters.

GET A QUOTE

Our Mission

To provide the most innovative high quality, cost effective risk control consultancy and survey services to support our clients mitigate and minimize the risks they face when undertaking marine transportation, offshore drilling and marine construction operations while executing their projects and marine operations in a timely and safe manner.

Our business focuses on all aspects of maritime transportation, construction / installation and drilling operations in the marine environment and upon the accidents and disputes that sometimes arise. Thanks to our multi-disciplinary team’s technical expertise and hands-on experience, we have become a recognised industry organisation in all of our fields of activity globally.

Our broad experience and our team's are highly valued by clients from almost every sector of the maritime & energy industries. These include maritime management and energy companies, P&I clubs, marine and energy insurers, ship owners and charterers, admiralty lawyers and government bodies. We develop long-standing relationships with our clients, who appreciate our professionalism, high level of safety, and above all our ability to respond quickly and efficiently to all urgent incidents.

Our Vision

To maintain our status as a reputable marine & engineering survey and consultancy organisation within the maritime and energy sectors recognised for our experience, the unsurpassed value of the service we provide and high quality skills of our people..
